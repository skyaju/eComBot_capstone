# Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping
- Delivery window: 5 to 7 business days after dispatch.
- Tracking updates become available once the parcel reaches the carrier network.

## Express Shipping
- Delivery window: 2 to 3 business days for eligible regions.
- Additional charges apply at checkout.

## Carrier Partners
Primary carriers include FedEx, UPS, and regional last-mile providers.

## Address Updates
Address changes are supported before fulfillment lock. Once shipped, changes require carrier-level rerouting and may incur delay.
