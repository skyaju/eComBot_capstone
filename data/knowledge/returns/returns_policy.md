# Returns and Refund Policy

## Return Window
Unused items can be returned within 30 days from the delivery date.

## Return Conditions
- Product must be in original condition.
- Accessories and included components should be returned together.

## Refund Timeline
Once inspection is approved, refunds are issued in 3 to 5 business days to the original payment method.

## Exceptions
Final-sale items and certain hygiene-sensitive products may be non-returnable unless defective.
