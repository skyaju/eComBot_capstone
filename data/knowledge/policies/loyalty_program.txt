Loyalty Program Overview

Members earn points on eligible purchases and can redeem points for discounts at checkout.

Core rules:
- Enrollment is free.
- Points are awarded after order completion.
- Promotional multipliers may apply during campaigns.
- Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity.

Tier benefits:
- Silver: early campaign access
- Gold: priority support and bonus point events
- Platinum: highest bonus rates and exclusive bundles
